About CleanFuture

Learn about our journey, mission, and the team behind our sustainable cleaning revolution.

Our Story

CleanFuture was founded in March of 2025 with a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize the cleaning industry through sustainable practices and innovative technology. What began as a small team of environmentally conscious professionals has grown into a leading cleaning service provider committed to making a positive impact.

Our journey started when our founder, Kazibwe Joseph, recognized a critical gap in the market. Traditional cleaning services relied heavily on harsh chemicals that were not only harmful to the environment but also posed health risks to clients and cleaning staff alike. Driven by a passion for sustainability and a belief that clean spaces should never come at the expense of our planet's health, Joseph assembled a team of like-minded professionals to create CleanFuture.

Currently we serve tens of residential and commercial clients across the metro area, consistently delivering exceptional cleaning results while staying true to our eco-friendly principles. Our growth is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.
